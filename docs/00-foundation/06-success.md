# Success

Success is not measured by downloads, stars, or feature count.

Radicant succeeds when engineers:

- Remember what they have learned.
- Reflect more often.
- Build with greater confidence.
- Share knowledge more effectively.
- Tell the story behind their work.
- Grow continuously throughout their careers.

If engineers feel more capable, more thoughtful, and more connected to their own journey because of Radicant, we have succeeded.
