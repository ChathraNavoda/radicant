# Brand Identity

## Brand Name

**Radicant**

> Set your roots in motion.

---

# Brand Essence

Radicant is a platform for engineers to document, understand, and showcase their professional growth.

It transforms careers into living systems rather than static résumés.

Instead of asking, "What have you built?", Radicant asks, "How have you grown?"

---

# Mission

Help every software engineer understand, preserve, and communicate the journey that shaped them.

---

# Vision

Become the professional identity platform every engineer builds throughout their career.

A place where knowledge, projects, experiences, and continuous learning become a living profile rather than a forgotten history.

---

# Brand Promise

Every software engineer should have a Radicant profile.

---

# Core Metaphor

Growth.

A radicant plant grows new roots as it moves forward.

Likewise, every project, lesson, challenge, and experience strengthens an engineer's foundation.

Knowledge is not collected.

Knowledge grows.

---

# Brand Personality

Radicant is:

- Calm
- Thoughtful
- Professional
- Honest
- Curious
- Disciplined
- Timeless

Radicant is not:

- Loud
- Trendy
- Flashy
- Gamified
- Overly corporate
- Artificially motivational

---

# Emotional Goal

Users should feel:

> "I can see how far I've come."

and

> "I want to keep growing."

---

# Audience

Primary audience:

Software engineers who value craftsmanship and continuous learning.

Future audiences:

- Engineering students
- Tech leads
- Hiring managers
- Engineering teams

---

# Positioning

Radicant is not another portfolio.

It is not another résumé.

It is not another productivity tool.

It is a living engineering growth platform.

---

# Brand Principles

- Growth over perfection.
- Depth over noise.
- Craftsmanship over speed.
- Reflection over metrics.
- Consistency over intensity.

---

# Success Statement

If someone opens a Radicant profile years from now, they should understand not only what an engineer built, but how they became who they are.
