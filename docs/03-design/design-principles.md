# Design Principles

## 1. Calm before clever

The interface should feel peaceful.

Avoid unnecessary decoration.

The product should let the user's work stand out.

---

## 2. Growth is visible

Every meaningful action should make progress visible.

Growth should be earned.

Never fake progress.

---

## 3. Structure creates confidence

Layouts should feel organized.

Whitespace is intentional.

Typography should lead the eye naturally.

---

## 4. Nature meets engineering

Organic inspiration.

Precise execution.

Roots, rings, trees, and branching structures should inspire—not dominate—the interface.

---

## 5. Timeless over trendy

Avoid design trends that will look dated in two years.

Aim for a product that still feels modern five years from now.
