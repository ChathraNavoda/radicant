# User Journey

## Trigger

The engineer solves a difficult problem.

↓

## Capture

Radicant prompts:

"What did you learn?"

↓

The engineer spends less than two minutes recording:

- Problem
- Solution
- Lesson
- Decision

↓

## Enrichment

AI automatically:

- summarizes
- extracts technologies
- links related knowledge
- suggests tags

↓

## Growth

Knowledge becomes part of the engineer's timeline.

↓

## Reuse

Months later...

The engineer searches.

Finds the lesson.

Uses it again.

↓

## Reflection

Growth becomes visible.

The engineer realizes how much they have learned.
