# MVP

## Goal

Validate that engineers are willing to build an engineering memory.

---

## Included

- Authentication
- Projects
- Journal Entries
- AI Summary
- Tags
- Search
- Timeline
- Reflections

---

## Excluded

- Teams
- Public Profiles
- Payments
- Notifications
- Community
- Advanced AI
- Portfolio Generator
- CV Generator

---

## Success Metrics

- 50 active users
- 500 journal entries
- 80% weekly retention
- Users create at least one entry per project
