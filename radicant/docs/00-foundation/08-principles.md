# Founding Principles

## 1. Solve real problems.

Build products that engineers genuinely need.

---

## 2. Preserve knowledge before it disappears.

Engineering knowledge is too valuable to lose.

---

## 3. Reflection is part of engineering.

Learning is incomplete without reflection.

---

## 4. Simplicity is a feature.

Every feature should reduce complexity rather than create it.

---

## 5. AI amplifies people.

AI exists to strengthen human thinking, not replace it.

---

## 6. Build for decades.

Choose timeless ideas over temporary trends.

---

## 7. Respect ownership.

Engineers own their knowledge.

Privacy and trust are fundamental.

---

## 8. Grow continuously.

Radicant should grow alongside its users.

---

## 9. Craft matters.

Quality is remembered long after speed is forgotten.

---

## 10. Build what we believe in.

We will never ship features we wouldn't use ourselves.
