# Brand

## Name

Radicant

## Pronunciation

RAD-ih-kənt

## Meaning

A radicant organism grows by continuously putting down new roots as it moves forward.

The name reflects the journey of every software engineer: continuously learning, adapting, and building stronger foundations throughout a career.

## Brand Promise

Help software engineers cultivate their engineering legacy.

## Personality

- Thoughtful
- Calm
- Intelligent
- Curious
- Trustworthy
- Timeless
- Human

## Tone of Voice

We speak with clarity rather than complexity.

We value craftsmanship over hype.

We educate rather than impress.

We inspire without exaggeration.

## Core Metaphor

Roots represent knowledge.

Projects become branches.

Growth forms the tree.

Legacy is the forest left behind.
