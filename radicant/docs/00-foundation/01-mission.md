# Mission

## Our Mission

Every software engineer deserves to remember how they became great.

Radicant exists to help software engineers capture, connect, and cultivate the knowledge behind their work.

We believe engineering is more than writing code.

Every project teaches lessons.

Every bug reveals insight.

Every architectural decision shapes judgment.

Every challenge becomes experience.

Too often, those moments disappear across repositories, documents, conversations, and memory.

Our mission is to ensure they never have to.

By helping engineers preserve and reflect on their journey, we aim to transform scattered experiences into lasting knowledge—and lasting knowledge into engineering wisdom.
